Vaccine Information

A preparation that is used to stimulate the body's immune response against diseases.

Engerix-B

Hepatitis B Vaccine

Manufacturer: GlaxoSmithKline (GSK)

Description: Engerix-B is a recombinant hepatitis B vaccine. It contains a surface antigen (HBsAg).

Disease Targeted: Hepatitis B

Storage Requirement: Should refrigerate at a specific temperature range

Side Effects: Mild and include pain or swelling at the injection site, low-grade fever, or fatigue.

Additional Notes: The Hepatitis B vaccine is often included in routine immunization schedules worldwide.

Daptacel

DTaP Vaccine

Manufacturer: Sanofi Pasteur

Description: Contains inactivated toxins or components of the bacteria that cause diphtheria, tetanus, and pertussis.

Diseases Targeted: a) Diphtheria b) Tetanus c) Pertussis

Storage Requirement: Should refrigerate at a specific temperature range

Side Effects: Mild and may include pain or swelling at the injection site, fever, and fussiness.

Additional Notes: DTaP vaccine is a standard childhood vaccine and is usually administered in a series of doses during infancy.
